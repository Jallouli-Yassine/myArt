Hi, I am Malek, a contemporary painter obsessed with expressing emotions through soft, pastel hues.

Since I was a little girl, colors have always been my best friends. I use mostly acrylics, watercolors, and occasionally gouache to build my dreamy little worlds.

My art aims to bring a fleeting sense of comfort, a warm hug in visual form. Thank you for taking the time to stop by my cozy corner of the internet. Let's make the world a cuter place, one painting at a time! 🎨🌸
